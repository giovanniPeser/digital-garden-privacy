Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 13, 2024

Digital Garden ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our Android application.

1. Data Collection & Storage

Local Storage: By default, your plant data, care schedules, and diary photos are stored locally on your device. We cannot access this data unless you enable cloud features.

Cloud Sync (Optional): If you create an account and enable "Cloud Sync," your garden data and plant photos are uploaded to our secure servers (hosted on Cloudflare) to allow multi-device synchronization and backup.

Authentication: We use Google Sign-In and email/password authentication. We store your email address and a unique User ID to manage your account and subscription status.

2. AI Processing

To provide features like Plant Identification and the AI Expert, the App sends relevant plant context and photos to our AI partners (including PlantNet and OpenRouter). This data is processed temporarily to generate results and is not used to build a profile about you.

3. Subscriptions & Payments

All premium subscriptions are processed securely through Google Play Billing. We do not store or have access to your credit card information or billing address.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated cloud data at any time through the App settings. Local data can be cleared by uninstalling the App or clearing App data in Android settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party providers:

  • Google Play Services: For authentication and billing.
  • Cloudflare: For secure data hosting and synchronization.
  • AdMob: To serve advertisements to non-premium users.
